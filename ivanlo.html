Who is BLACKPINK?

BLACKPINK, a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, made their official debut on August 8, 2016, with the digital singles "Boombayah" and "Whistle." Comprising four members, Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa, the group swiftly gained international acclaim for their distinctive music, captivating performances, and visually appealing music videos. Each member contributes unique talents to the group, with Jisoo as the main visual and lead vocalist, Jennie as the main rapper and lead vocalist, Rosé as the main vocalist and lead dancer, and Lisa as the main dancer and lead rapper. BLACKPINK's success transcended national borders, and they achieved record-breaking milestones, including becoming the first K-pop girl group to perform at Coachella in 2019. Their discography is marked by hit songs such as "DDU-DU DDU-DU," "Kill This Love," "How You Like That," and "Lovesick Girls." The group's global impact extends beyond music, with members participating in solo projects, fashion campaigns, and collaborations with international artists, solidifying their status as not only K-pop icons but also influential figures in the global entertainment industry.

Members

JISOO

Birth Name: Kim Ji Soo
Position: Lead Vocalist, Main Visual
Birthday: January 3, 1995
Zodiac Sign: Capricorn
Birthplace: Seoul, South Korea
Height: 162cm
Weight: 45 kg (99 lbs)
Jisoo, who trained for five years starting in July 2011, has an older brother and an older sister. Fluent in Korean, Chinese, and Japanese, she is skilled in playing the drums and the piano, enjoys playing Overwatch with Jennie, and loves the color purple. Jisoo has shared her passion for capturing moments through various posts on social media, showcasing her talent behind the camera and giving fans a glimpse into her creative side beyond her musical endeavors. She has also ventured into the realm of acting in "Arthdal Chronicles," where she showcased her versatility by taking on a supporting role. Jisoo has further solidified her presence in the acting scene by securing a leading role in the drama "Snowdrop." Her foray into acting demonstrates her commitment to expanding her artistic horizons and exploring diverse forms of expression beyond the music stage.

JENNIE

Birth Name: Jennie Kim
Position: Main Rapper, Lead Vocalist
Birthday: January 16, 1996
Zodiac Sign: Capricorn
Birthplace: Seoul, South Korea
Height: 163cm
Weight: 50 kg (110 lbs)
Jennie trained for six years starting in August 2010. She is fluent in Korean, Japanese, and English, and is known for her love of fashion. Jennie has a passion for fashion and has been recognized as a style icon, with her distinctive sense of style and trendsetting looks garnering attention from the fashion industry. Jennie has showcased her versatility through solo projects, including her debut single "SOLO," which achieved commercial success and further established her as a multifaceted artist. Jennie was the first member publicly revealed in the group, earning the endearing nickname "the YG princess." Known for her impeccable fashion sense, she is affectionately called Human Chanel due to her penchant for donning luxurious clothing from the brand.

ROSÉ

Birth Name: Park Chae Young (Roseanne Park)
Position: Main Vocalist, Lead Dancer
Birthday: February 11, 1997
Zodiac Sign: Aquarius
Birthplace: New Zealand
Height: 168cm
Weight: 45 kg (99 lbs)
Born in Auckland, New Zealand, and raised in Melbourne, Box Hill, Australia, Rosé, a Korean with a rich cultural background, shares her upbringing with her older sister, Alice. In 2012, she made a significant move to Korea, as disclosed during Weekly Idol, emerging as the final member introduced in the group BLACKPINK. Rosé's linguistic versatility extends to her fluency in Korean, English, and Japanese, complementing her exceptional vocal talent and distinct features. Renowned for her unique voice, she is also recognized for her slender waist, with her stylist revealing her pants size as 24 and her waist measuring less than 24 inches (60.96 cm). Beyond her musical prowess, Rosé is a skilled musician, proficient in playing both the piano and the guitar. Adding to her uniqueness, she is left-handed, showcasing a distinctive aspect of her personality.

LISA

Birth Name: Lalisa Manoban / Pranpriya Manoban
Position: Main Dancer, Lead Rapper, Sub-Vocalist
Birthday: March 27, 1997
Zodiac Sign: Aries
Birthplace: Bangkok, Thailand
Height: 167cm
Weight: 46 kg (101 lbs)
Lisa, accepted as the sole candidate in the YG Audition in Thailand in 2010, shares a childhood friendship with GOT7's Bambam, having been part of the dance crew We Zaa Cool. Training for five years and three months starting in April 2011, Lisa, the second member unveiled, showcases her linguistic prowess in Korean, English, Japanese, and Thai. Known as the "Thailand Princess" in her birthplace, Lisa's playful and mischievous demeanor off-stage, along with her acclaimed aegyo skills, contribute to her dynamic presence within BLACKPINK. Lisa holds the distinction of being the most-followed K-pop idol on Instagram, amassing millions of followers. Her social media presence allows her to connect directly with fans worldwide, and her posts often garner immense attention. Lisa has received international recognition for her contributions to music and fashion.
